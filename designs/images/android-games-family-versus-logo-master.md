## Modules

1. **Larger player:** gender-neutral circular head and one rounded shoulder/arm arc on the left, facing inward.
2. **Smaller player:** matching abstract construction on the right at about 82% scale, facing inward, with comparable optical weight.
3. **Shared game table:** one compact rounded-square field between them with only one horizontal line, one vertical line, and two equal-size opposing round pieces.
4. **Equal-play relationship:** both players reach the table by the same visual distance; the two game pieces are exactly equal in diameter.
