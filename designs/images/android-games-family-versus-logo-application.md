## Approved source override

All application samples must use the complete approved 1254×1254 artwork without redrawing, recoloring, cropping, moving individual forms, or replacing its background. Android icon density variants are mechanical equal-aspect downscales of root `logo.svg`.

## Layout

- Clean landscape brand-application board with three clearly separated, unlabeled zones.
- Left: one large Android launcher icon.
- Upper right: the logo at small top-bar scale beside a neutral title placeholder.
- Lower right: monochrome ink and inverse compact variants at 48dp-equivalent scale.
- Keep generous spacing so each use can be judged independently.

## Modules

1. **Launcher icon:** deep teal rounded-square field with a single porcelain version of the parent-child face-to-face game mark inside the mask-safe center.
2. **Top-bar lockup:** full-color flat mark at 36–40dp-equivalent scale followed by one dark neutral text bar; no rendered words.
3. **Compact variants:** a one-color deep-ink mark on porcelain and a one-color porcelain mark on deep teal.

## Visual details

- Presentation background: mineral grey `#DDE5E3`.
- Launcher: solid teal `#185864` surface, porcelain `#F5F2E9` foreground, no internal shadow.
- Top-bar mark: teal larger player, maple smaller player, ink table.
- Compact marks: one color only; simplify the table to a cross intersection and two equal pieces if needed.
- The mark remains crisp at small size; all critical gaps stay visibly open.
- Only the launcher tile may have a subtle porcelain-like rim; the logo itself stays flat.
