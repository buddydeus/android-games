# international-chess-ui - menu

**Preview file:** `designs/previews/international-chess-ui-menu.png`
**Spec SSOT:** `designs/specs/international-chess-ui.md`

## Layout

Landscape Android tablet UI at 2560 x 1600. A large square chessboard occupies the left two-thirds. A single full-height score-sheet panel occupies the right side. The complete initial position is visible with White at the bottom.

## Modules

- Mineral background with quiet cool-grey texture.
- Graphite-green stone board rim and an 8 x 8 muted tournament board.
- Twelve-style family represented by individually rendered porcelain and obsidian Staunton pieces.
- Right menu rail with title, English utility subtitle, version, and three vertically grouped mode buttons.

## Visual details

- Follow the exact tokens in the SSOT.
- Use a restrained system serif only for `国际象棋`; all other text is system sans.
- Board and pieces carry the visual identity; the rail is matte and quiet.
- Buttons are rectangular with 8 dp radius, clear borders, and no floating cards.
- No warm wood, leather, neon, gradient glow, heraldry, or ornate medieval decoration.

## Image prompt

Use case: ui-mockup
Asset type: high-fidelity Android tablet game main-menu preview
Primary request: Design the main menu of an offline international chess game for a shared Android tablet.
Scene/backdrop: cool daylight mineral-grey app background, no environment outside the UI.
Composition/framing: exact 16:10 landscape screen, large square chessboard on the left two-thirds, one full-height matte score-sheet menu rail on the right, no nested cards.
Board: graphite-green mineral stone rim, alternating neutral grey-ivory #D8D4C6 and muted tournament green #557161 squares, subtle coordinates, complete initial chess position with White at the bottom.
Pieces: modern Staunton chess pieces rendered as consistent physical PNG-style assets; White is matte ivory porcelain with graphite edges, Black is soft-black obsidian with restrained cool rim light; same slightly elevated front/top three-quarter camera angle; pieces stay fully inside squares.
Rail text (verbatim Chinese): "国际象棋", "CLASSICAL BOARD", "版本 0.0.7", "单人模式", "双人对战", "退出游戏".
Typography: Android system serif for the main title only, Android system sans for all other text, zero letter spacing.
Controls: three 8px-radius rectangular touch buttons, first deep graphite-green with white text, second outlined neutral, third quiet red outline.
Lighting/mood: calm daylight, tactile but restrained, tournament precision, premium offline family game.
Constraints: readable exact Chinese text, stable square board geometry, no text overlap, no cropped pieces, no floating card around the primary board, no marketing copy.
Avoid: brown wooden chessboard, leather, fantasy ornaments, heraldry, crowns as decoration, neon, glassmorphism, glowing gradients, purple, dark game lobby, exaggerated shadows, watermark.
