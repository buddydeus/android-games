# android-games-home — mobile

**Preview file:** `designs/previews/android-games-home-mobile.png`
**Spec SSOT:** `designs/specs/android-games-home.md`

## Layout

Straight-on 9:16 portrait Android compact screen without a device frame. A compact top app bar sits above “选择游戏”. Three full-width equal-height tactile buttons are stacked with equal gaps. Every button uses the same 64dp recessed Logo well and the same text alignment.

## Modules

- Top app bar: exact title “游戏中心”; compact outlined action “导入”.
- Section title: exact text “选择游戏”.
- Three equal 112dp-feeling horizontal buttons:
  - Five-stone Logo + “五子棋”.
  - Circular “象” piece Logo + “象棋”.
  - Black/white flipping-disc Logo + “黑白棋”.

## Visual details

Use the same mineral canvas, matte porcelain surface, visible outline, white rim and double-shadow system as desktop. Keep tactile depth modest enough that text and boundaries remain high contrast. No button receives a unique surface, size, radius or elevation. Logo shape—not container styling—identifies the game.
