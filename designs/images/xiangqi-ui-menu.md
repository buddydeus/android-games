# Xiangqi UI - Menu

**Preview file:** `designs/previews/xiangqi-ui-menu.png`
**Spec SSOT:** `designs/specs/xiangqi-ui.md`

## Layout

Landscape Android Pad UI at 1600 x 1000. The complete 9 x 10 board dominates the left two-thirds. A flat, full-height score-sheet rail occupies the right third with generous negative space and three large controls.

## Modules

- **Porcelain board:** complete orthographic pale board, celadon rim, ink grid, palace diagonals, position marks, and pale celadon river band.
- **Initial pieces:** all 32 round pieces placed at intersections using the matched ivory/cinnabar and ivory/blue-black texture family.
- **Menu rail:** title `象棋`, subtitle `楚河 · 漢界`, version label, and buttons `单人模式`, `双人对战`, `退出游戏`.
- **Backdrop:** quiet bright mineral-celadon field with one or two subtle horizontal rules, not a framed card.

## Visual Details

Use `#EAF1EF` canvas, `#F5F1E8` board, `#A9C7BE` rim, `#263C38` grid, `#D9E9E4` river, and `#FBFCFA` rail. The title uses restrained Song-style serif; all operational text uses a clean Android sans. Buttons are 54 dp high with 7 dp radius. The board and pieces carry all material depth; the rail is flat.
